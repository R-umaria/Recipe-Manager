Ingredients: 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breast thinly sliced, 2 cups broccoli florets, 1 bell pepper thinly sliced, 3 tablespoons soy sauce, 1 tablespoon sesame oil, 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
Step1: Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Add sliced chicken and cook until browned.
Step2: Add broccoli and bell pepper to the pan. Stir-fry until vegetables are tender-crisp.
Step3: In a small bowl, mix soy sauce and sesame oil. Pour the sauce over the chicken and vegetables. Stir to coat evenly. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
