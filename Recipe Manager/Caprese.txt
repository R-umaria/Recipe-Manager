Ingredients: 4 large tomatoes sliced, 1 pound fresh mozzarella sliced, Fresh basil leaves, Balsamic glaze, Salt and pepper to taste.
Step1: Arrange tomato and mozzarella slices on a platter, alternating and overlapping.
Step2: Tuck fresh basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices.
Step3: Drizzle with balsamic glaze and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.
